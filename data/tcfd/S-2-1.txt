S-2-1
公司應進一步揭露所鑑別氣候相關的風險與機會是如何影響其業務、策略及財務規劃 評估，以及對下列領域業務和策略的影響： 
 ● 產品與服務 
 ● 供應鏈或價值鏈 
 ● 調適與減緩活動 
 ● 研發投資 
 ● 業務經營（包括業務類型和設施所在地）。
 Building on recommended disclosure (a), organizations should discuss how identified climate-related issues have affected their businesses, strategy.
 Organizations should consider including the impact on their businesses, strategy, and financial planning in the following areas:
 ‒ Products and services
 ‒ Supply chain and/or value chain
 ‒ Adaptation and mitigation activities
 ‒ Investment in research and development
 ‒ Operations (including types of operations and location of facilities)
